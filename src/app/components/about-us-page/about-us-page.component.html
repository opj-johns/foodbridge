Who We Are

We are a dedicated team committed to ensuring that students have access to the food they need to thrive. By connecting generous donors, local businesses, and our community, we empower students to purchase essential food items from partner shops. Our mission is to create a sustainable system that supports students' well-being and helps them with their academic journey.

Our Mission

Address food insecurity among university students by providing registered FoodBridge members with the means to purchase the essential food items they need each month.

Our Vision

A future where all students have access to the food they need to thrive, ensuring no one faces hunger while pursuing their education.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, our NGO was born from the belief that no one should have to face hunger. Over the years, we’ve partnered with local businesses, caring individuals, and communities to create a food distribution network that bridges the gap between abundance and scarcity.
