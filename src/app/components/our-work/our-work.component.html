How We Fight Hunger Together

We connect local businesses with caring individuals to fight hunger. Businesses register people in need, and donors can purchase meals directly from home. Together, we’re making it easy to provide food for those who need it most.

Local stores/shops register

Local stores sign up to provide food items to help students in need.

Donors and Sponsors

Generous donors can contribute funds directly to us, which will then be transferred to local shops to support students in purchasing the food they need.

Students Register

Registered students can visit partner shops to purchase food items up to a specified value, ensuring they receive the necessary supplies.
